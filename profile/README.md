## 🖌 Overview  
Movist Pro Advanced is a high-performance video player that handles virtually any video format with hardware-accelerated decoding, delivering smooth, high-quality playback while keeping CPU usage minimal through GPU-accelerated processing on Apple Silicon and Intel hardware.

Subtitle support is among the most comprehensive available in any media player, covering multiple subtitle tracks, ASS and SSA advanced subtitle formatting, subtitle delay adjustment, and detailed typography customization that gives you precise control over how subtitles appear.

Format compatibility spans the full range of video containers and codecs including MKV, AVI, MP4, FLV, and dozens of others, handling complex files with multiple audio and subtitle tracks without the compatibility limitations of more restricted players.

Audio track and channel management gives you full control over multi-audio files, letting you select language tracks, configure surround sound output, and adjust channel levels for the listening setup you are using at any given moment.

For video enthusiasts, subtitle readers, and users who regularly work with diverse video files from international sources, Movist Pro Advanced is the media player that handles every file correctly with the quality and control the content deserves.

## 🚀 Why Choose Movist Pro Advanced?

Movist Pro Advanced represents the perfect balance between powerful functionality and elegant simplicity. Built specifically for Apple Silicon, this application takes full advantage of cutting-edge technologies including Metal graphics acceleration, Grand Central Dispatch for multi-threading, and native components that ensure buttery-smooth performance even on older hardware.

The application has been meticulously optimized for both Intel-based systems and the latest Apple Silicon chips, including M1, M2, M3, and M4 processors. Whether you are running older hardware or the latest devices, you will experience the same level of responsiveness and efficiency.

What truly sets Movist Pro Advanced apart is its commitment to user privacy and data security. All processing happens locally on your device, with no data ever sent to external servers. Your documents and work remain completely private and under your control.

Regular updates bring not just bug fixes, but meaningful feature enhancements based on real user feedback. The development team actively listens to the community and implements requested improvements, ensuring the software evolves alongside your needs.

The application seamlessly integrates with your existing workflow, supporting drag-and-drop functionality, Quick Look previews, Spotlight searching, and even Shortcuts automation for power users.
